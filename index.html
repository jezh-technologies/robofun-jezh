About Robofun

"Welcome to our exciting Robofun workshop designed for school students! In this workshop, we take students on a journey into the fascinating worlds of robotics and artificial intelligence (AI). Through engaging presentations, hands-on activities, and group discussions, students will discover the fundamentals of robotics and AI, learn to assemble and program basic robots, and explore the practical applications of these technologies. Our workshop fosters creativity and problem-solving, allowing students to work on both minor activities and major projects that showcase their newfound knowledge. Join us for a fun and educational experience that ignites a passion for technology and innovation!"
